Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Rare Pigeons is a collection of 10,000 unique digital pigeon NFTs stored on the Ethereum blockchain. Each pigeon is algorithmically generated from hundreds of hand-drawn traits, making every single one completely unique.

Connect your MetaMask or WalletConnect wallet, select the number of pigeons you want to mint (up to 5 per transaction), and click "Mint". The cost is 0.08 ETH per pigeon plus gas fees.

Rarity is revealed instantly upon minting. You'll know your pigeon's tier — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, or Legendary — as soon as the transaction is confirmed.

Rare Pigeons are minted on the Ethereum blockchain as ERC-721 tokens. This ensures full compatibility with all major NFT marketplaces like OpenSea, Blur, and LooksRare.

Holders get access to the exclusive Rare Pigeons Discord, future airdrops, voting rights on collection decisions, real-world events (Legendary holders), merch store access, and commercial rights to use their pigeon's artwork.

Yes. The Rare Pigeons smart contract has been fully audited by a leading blockchain security firm. The audit report is publicly available on our GitHub.

A 5% royalty is applied to all secondary sales. 3% goes to the team for continued development, and 2% is donated to avian conservation charities worldwide.
