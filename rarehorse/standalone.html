Rare Horses is a collection of 10,000 unique digital horse NFTs stored on the Ethereum blockchain. Each horse is algorithmically generated from hundreds of hand-drawn traits, making every single one completely unique.

Connect your MetaMask or WalletConnect wallet, select the number of horses you want to mint (up to 5 per transaction), and click "Mint". The cost is 0.08 ETH per horse plus gas fees.

Rarity is revealed instantly upon minting. You'll know your horse's tier ‚Äî Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, or Legendary ‚Äî as soon as the transaction is confirmed.

Rare Horses are minted on the Ethereum blockchain as ERC-721 tokens. This ensures full compatibility with all major NFT marketplaces like OpenSea, Blur, and LooksRare.

Holders get access to the exclusive Rare Horses Discord, future airdrops, voting rights on collection decisions, real-world events (Legendary holders), merch store access, and commercial rights to use their horse's artwork.
